# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful in disagreements
- giving and accepting constructive feedback
- assuming good intent while still being precise
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- respecting different experience levels and communication styles

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, threats, or intimidation
- insulting or derogatory comments
- publishing private information without permission
- sustained disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions
- sexualized language or imagery

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, and other communication channels directly tied to the
project.

## Enforcement

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through GitHub issues
or repository contact channels. Reports will be reviewed and handled in good
faith.
